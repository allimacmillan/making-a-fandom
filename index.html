Background

Shane Dawson is considered to be an "original youtuber," meaning he has been on the platform for much longer than many of the current popular youtubers. He started on the platform in 2008, making mostly short comedy sketch videos. Later, he became very popular from his conspiracy theory videos. Some are believable and very true, while others are a little more far-fetched. Recently, he has had success with a new format of content: docu-series. One popular series that he created is an in-depth look at the life of Jake Paul, a younger popular YouTuber. Dawson has now done several of these docu-series-style videos and they have brought him great success. The most recent of these is the "Conspiracy Theories with Shane Dawson" 2-part series. He now lives in Los Angeles with his boyfriend Ryland (who is also a youtuber), their two dogs, and cat. He has amassed over 20 million subscribers on YouTube.

Video Style

Dawson is known for his unique energy and editing style. He includes quick inserts of images and sounds and talks in a very animated voice. He always uses dramatic background music to make the scenario seem more intense. He seems to find the drama and, as he likes to call it, "tea" in every possibly sketchy situation. He and his videographer Andrew Siwicki do a really good job of finding clips of other YouTubers, news clips, and any outside video they can find that will support their narrative. His more recent style of creating YouTube docu-series' is incredibly focused on the narrative of the story instead of simply one video about topics with no real story progression. This new narrative version of video has been incredibly successful for Dawson, with his most-watched video now at 35 million views.
